GENERAL GUIDELINES

  • All delegates are expected to maintain decorum when inside school premises and follow instructions given by their respective Executive Board members whilst in active committee session.
  • All delegates must arrive at 8:30 am and be present in committee during all sessions.
  • Students from grade 8 to 12 can participate, with the exception of Lok Sabha, which is open only for grade 10 to 12
  • Lunch will not be provided by the school, students are encouraged to carry their own food. Evening Snacks/Light Refreshments will be provided to delegates.
  • WhatsApp/Instagram/other social media groups not created or reccomended by schools must not be used by participating delegates
  • Registration fee for delegates is Rs. 500
